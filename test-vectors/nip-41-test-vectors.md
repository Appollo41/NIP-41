## Bridge fixtures

These vectors exercise the spec's bridge mechanism, which lets a
legacy (uncommitted) key migrate **once** into a fresh committed
chain. The legacy key and the fresh chain are independent of the
rotation chain above; reusing values across the two would make the
narrative ambiguous (a key is either a chain head OR a bridge
target, never both in the same world line).
